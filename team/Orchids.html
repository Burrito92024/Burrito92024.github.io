Orchids

Orchids belong to the family of Orchidaceae. Orchid colors vary greatly, the most common colors are red, orange, yellow, pink, white, and purple, and the rarer colors are blue and green. The orchid most often seen in stores and markets is the Moth Orchid. Orchids are characterized by their symmetrical petals, the blooms are bilateral, meaning each side is symmetrical to the one vertical, or across, from it. Orchids are a great beginner plant.
